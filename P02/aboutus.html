About Us


Book People is one of the best book retailers in the Greater Vancouver Area. The shop is small but the stock is carefully selected. There’s a mix of new releases and old, as well as fresh books and slightly used. We carry a large selection of current paperback best sellers in all major categories. We are made up of a large team of book lovers and are passionate about publishing, reading, and writing and want to make sure that you find what you're looking for. Allow Book People to be the people that help you.
